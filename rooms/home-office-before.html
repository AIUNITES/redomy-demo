Why this room exists

Most renovation visualizers show pristine rooms. That is the wrong demo for cable management because the value of the renovation only shows up against the chaos. This room is built to look like a real working developer/maker desk: tangled bundles, power strips on the surface, a drawer of unsorted spares.

Each clickable object pairs the problem with its fix and a recommended product class. The companion After room shows the same desk after applying every fix, so you can compare what each change actually buys you.

Cable management for a workbench is a different problem from cable management for an executive desk. The goal is not invisibility; it is identification and access. Every cable labeled, every outlet switchable, spare cables hung by category. A working lab can show 50 visible cables and still feel under control.
